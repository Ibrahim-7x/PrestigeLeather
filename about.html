SINCE 1985

Crafting Excellence, Stitch by Stitch

Founded in the heart of Italy's leather district, Prestige Leather has been the pinnacle of motorcycle leather craftsmanship for over four decades. What began as a small family workshop has evolved into a globally recognized brand, trusted by professional racers and enthusiasts alike.

Our journey started with a simple belief: that riders deserve gear that offers uncompromising protection without sacrificing style. This philosophy drives every stitch, every cut, and every design decision we make.

Marco Prestigiacomo, Founder

THE PROCESS

Artisan Craftsmanship

Each Prestige Leather suit undergoes a meticulous 47-step production process, taking approximately 40 hours to complete. From selecting the finest full-grain leather to the final quality inspection, no detail is overlooked.

01
Material Selection

Only the top 5% of leather hides make the cut. We source from ethical tanneries in Italy and France.

02
Pattern Cutting

Precision cutting ensures optimal grain direction and minimal waste while maximizing protection.

03
Assembly & Stitching

Double and triple stitching in high-stress areas. Kevlar-reinforced seams for added durability.

04
Armor Integration

CE-certified armor strategically placed at shoulders, elbows, knees, and hips.
