Leather Care Instructions

Cleaning

Wipe down with a damp cloth after each use. Use a leather-specific cleaner for deeper cleaning. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive materials.

Conditioning

Apply leather conditioner every 3-6 months to maintain suppleness. This prevents cracking and extends the life of your suit.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Use a padded hanger to maintain shape. Avoid plastic bags - use a breathable garment bag.

Water Exposure

If the suit gets wet, let it air dry naturally at room temperature. Never use heat sources like radiators or hair dryers.
