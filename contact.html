FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick Answers to Common Questions

We provide a detailed size guide on each product page. Measure your chest, waist, hips, and height, then compare to our sizing chart. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit. For custom sizing, contact our customer service team.

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in original packaging. Custom orders are non-returnable unless there's a manufacturing defect. Return shipping is free for exchanges. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the return.

Yes! We offer made-to-measure custom suits for riders who need a perfect fit. Custom orders take 4-6 weeks to complete and start at $2,500. Contact our custom department at custom@prestigeleather.com for a consultation.

Standard shipping (5-7 business days) is free worldwide. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is $29.99. Overnight shipping is available for $49.99. Custom orders have their own timeline communicated during the ordering process.

Clean with a damp cloth after each use. Apply leather conditioner every 3-6 months. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight on a padded hanger. Never use heat to dry a wet suit - let it air dry naturally. See our Care Guide for detailed instructions.
